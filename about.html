Hospital Introduction

A premium care experience built around trust and clinical precision

Samrijo Hospital serves patients across Burari and greater Delhi with a focused approach to laser, laparoscopic, and general surgical care. Our environment is designed to feel organized, calm, and professional, helping patients move from diagnosis to recovery with clarity and confidence.

From first consultation to follow-up, our team emphasizes transparent communication, patient safety protocols, responsive care coordination, and a treatment philosophy that respects both medical excellence and human comfort.

Founder

MBBS, MS Surgery

He is known for taking time to explain conditions, help patients understand their options, and guide them through surgery and recovery with confidence. This balance of expertise and accessibility reflects the culture of care throughout the hospital.
